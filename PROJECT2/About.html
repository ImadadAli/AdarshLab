We have three types that appeal to schooling set ups. The mobile lab is on caster wheels and moves from one classroom to another is an ideal tool for science learners in a rural, township or urban school without much room for a conventional lab. A container and prefab labs are for those schools that have small portions of land that cannot accommodate a normal classroom, and we use the limited land to erect a structure, either container or prefab to install a furnished laboratory.
